Sandwich

Description

Is it a triangle? Or is it a circle? Maybe its a rectangle? Whatever shape it may be, once it is in yo tummy. You'll never regret it. A crunchy outside, and a fulfilling inside. Yummmmmm. Or Yuck? ALl depends on the person who made that sandwich.

Ingredients

  • Loft Bread
  • Lettuce
  • Choice of Meat: Ham, chicken, beef, salmon, you name it
  • Sear-pan or Toaster
  • Optional: Cheese

Steps

  1. Toast Bun
  2. Assemble sandwich with desired ingredients
  3. To make it more flavorful, add mayo/ketchup. The sky is the limit
  4. Slice diagonally and serve
